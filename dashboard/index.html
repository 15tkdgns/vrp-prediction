ğŸ¥ Comprehensive Model Diagnosis

Root Cause Analysis

1. Financial Data Characteristics

Key Finding: Stock market prediction is fundamentally challenging due to the efficient market hypothesis and low signal-to-noise ratio in financial data.

  • Market regime changes (COVID-19, inflation, AI boom) invalidate historical patterns
  • Noise-to-signal ratio is extremely high in financial time series
  • Pattern persistence is limited by market adaptation

2. Data Snooping Bias

Multiple model testing on the same dataset led to selection of accidentally successful patterns:

  • Tested 5+ different architectures on same data
  • Selected best-performing model (59.9% LSTM)
  • Pattern likely worked by chance on historical data
  • Failed to generalize to true out-of-sample data

3. Feature Redundancy

Too many correlated technical indicators created noise:

  • 21+ technical indicators with high intercorrelation
  • Multiple timeframes of same indicator (MA5, MA10, MA20)
  • Interaction terms amplified noise rather than signal

Standard cross-validation fails in financial data due to temporal dependencies:
